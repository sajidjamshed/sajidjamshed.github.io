Magic Drop - Phenyal


Liquid phenyl is used as strong deodorant & germicide for disinfection of areas like toilets, lavatory, hospital, educational places, offices, shops, drains, cowsheds, nursing homes etc and is widely used for sanitation purpose.


Types of Phenyl: The main types of Phenyl are:


Black Phenyl: It is a very powerful disinfectant usually in black or dark brown colour. The special feature of this phenyl is that it is effective in even very dilute state. That's why it is economically beneficial for large facilities to keep stock of black phenyl concentrate and then dilute it with water when to be used. Black phenyle is mostly used in hospitals, military facilities, homes, hotels, offices, and animal farms, among other places.


White Phenyl: It is usually perfumed one with white or milky colour and is made from pine oil. This disinfectant usually helps in removing odours and killing bacteria. White phenyle is a disinfecting agent made from pine oil. It is made by using an emulsifier, which is a compound that allows an oil to stably make a solution with water.


Green Phenyl: This disinfectant is used in eliminating insects from homes and is nontoxic, biodegradable and environment friendly. DGM residue is used as one of the raw materials in its preparation. Green phenyl is also neem-based phenyl made from a unique cold process technology. Phenyl also comes in other colors based on the fragrance like pink, yellow etc.
