Magic Drop - Bleach


Bleach is the generic name for any chemical product that is used industrially and domestically to remove colour from a fabric or fiber or to clean or to remove stains in a process called bleaching. It often refers, specifically, to a dilute solution of sodium hypochlorite, also called "liquid bleach".


Many bleaches have broad spectrum bactericidal properties, making them useful for disinfecting and sterilizing and are used in swimming pool, mosques, hotels, houses, offices etc to sanitation & control bacteria, viruses, germs and algae and in many places where sterile conditions are required and necessary.
