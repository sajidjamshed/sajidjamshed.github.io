About Us

Arooj International is a multinational organization operating in Europe, Middle East and North America. The company has just started its operations in Pakistan and envisioning to be among the leading FMCG companies of Pakistan.

In future, Arooj International is interested to introduce some new products that are not launced in Pakistan till now. Arooj International is determine to achieve its goals soon under its visionary management and experienced staff.



Pakistan is the fifth-most populous country of the world, with a population exceeding 216.6 million (2019). In order to create and build a strong customer base in such a widespread and diverse marketplace, Arooj International is offering a range of premium quality products under the logo Magic Drop , providing washing solutions. The products include Dish Wash, Tiolet Cleaner, Washing Cleaner, Bleach, Phenyal and so on. Arooj International is optimistic to mark its name in Pakistan that is one of the largest consumer economies around the world.
